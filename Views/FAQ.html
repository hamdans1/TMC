F.A.Q.

  • What is a Paint and Prosecco Party?

    • A PPP is a great night in with friends to paint and catch up! We supply everything you need to paint a custom designed board, you provide the Prosecco.

  • Where do I choose a design?

    • You create the unique design for your board. Send us your inspiration from wherever you get it, you tell us the story you want to tell with your board, our designers create something specific for you.

  • When do I choose my colors?

    • Unless you've decided to build a Porch Board, your project colors will be chosen the night of the party! Even if you decided to add a frame to your board, all of your colors will be chosen on the night of.

  • When does my design need to be finalized?

    • We need finalized designs at least two weeks before the date of your party. If you're a late addition to the party, you can still choose from one of our pre-stenciled outlines, and have all the painting fun with everyone else!

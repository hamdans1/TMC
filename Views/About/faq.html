What is a Paint & Prosecco Party?

A PPP is a great night in with friends to paint & catch up! We supply everything you need to paint a custom designed board, you supply the Prosecco.

Where are the designs to choose from?

We do not have designs to choose from, you create that one of a kind project that you have been wanting and couldn’t find at a store! Send us inspiration from Pinterest, Google, Etsy, or tell us a story of what you are looking for!

When will I choose my colors?

Your project colors will be chosen the night of the party! (except porch boards) If you have decided to frame your board you will choose that color the night of the party as well! (more information on frames in a little while)

I am not artistic, help!

Good! We are not artistic either, and you will still go home with a great project!

When does my design need to be finalized

14 days before your party. If it’s less than 14 days you will have the option to paint our LI Outline that says meet me by the water.

How do I pay?

Venmo App- if you do not have Venmo please let us know and we can accept cash or check at the party.
